## 🧠 Phase Details

### Phase 1: Foundation (10 minutes)

**What You'll Learn:**
- What is AIOX (AI-Orchestrated System for Full Stack Development)
- 13 agents and their roles
- Core concepts (Constitution, Story-Driven, Authority, Layers)
- Framework structure

**Key Takeaway:** AIOX is specialized agents with strict authority boundaries, rigid foundation + creative freedom.

### Phase 2: Mental Maps (15 minutes)

**What You'll Learn:**
- 8 DNA patterns (Agent Specialization, Story-Driven, Quality Gates, Authority Hierarchy, Layer Immutability, No Invention, IDS, Task-First)
- 6 Constitution articles (all non-negotiable)
- Authority hierarchy visualization
- Thinking patterns for decision-making

**Key Takeaway:** Internalize the philosophy. When unsure, the Constitution tells you the right answer.

### Phase 3: Processing Maps (20 minutes)

**What You'll Learn:**
- Story Development Cycle: 5 phases with gates
- Spec Pipeline: 6-phase requirement transformation
- QA Loop: Iterative review-fix
- Brownfield Discovery: Technical debt assessment
- 7 mandatory quality checks
- Workflow decision tree

**Key Takeaway:** Workflows are rigid. Follow them exactly. Adaptation comes WITHIN the phases, not around them.

### Phase 4: Mastery (25 minutes)

**What You'll Learn:**
- Real-world scenario 1: Story creation flow
- Real-world scenario 2: Authority enforcement
- Real-world scenario 3: Workflow selection
- Real-world scenario 4: Boundary violation handling
- Self-assessment questions
- Next steps for deep expertise

**Key Takeaway:** You can now navigate real AIOX production scenarios confidently.
