## Key Findings

- **EU Regulation Driver:** 2026 Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation bans destroying unsold goods → forces manufacturer liquidation
- **Market Size:** €644B global liquidation market (2025) → €700B+ (2026)
- **MOQ Reality:** Electronics liquidation works on **pallet basis** (typically 1 pallet = €380-€1,700), NOT per-unit like manufacturer wholesale (which requires 500-1000 units)
- **Typical €300 budget:** Covers 15-25 mixed units per pallet, NOT full pallets. Need to clarify with suppliers if fractional pallet available or if €300 is too small for entry
