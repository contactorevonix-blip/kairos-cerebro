## Model 1: Retail Arbitrage (Amazon FBA) — RECOMMENDED

### Entry & Operating Costs

```

MONTHLY OPERATING:

  Amazon seller subscriptions:         €0-40 (professional plan)

  MONTHLY TOTAL:                       €30-90
```

### Margin Breakdown (Per Product)

```
Example: Buy electronics at clearance, resell on Amazon

COST SIDE:
  Product cost:                        €10
  Inbound shipping to FBA:             €0.50
  FBA fulfillment fee (by volume):     €2.00
  TOTAL PRODUCT COST:                  €12.50

REVENUE SIDE:
  Sell price (market rate):            €25-30
  Amazon take (commission):            -€3.75 (15%)
  Payment processing:                  -€0.75 (2.9%)
  NET PER UNIT:                        €8-10

MARGIN CALCULATION:
  Gross margin: (€25-€12.50) / €25 = 50%
  Net margin: €8 / €25 = 32%
```

### Real Market Data (2024-2026)

| Metric | Low | Typical | High |
|--------|-----|---------|------|
| **Net margin per product** | 12% | 20% | 30% |

**Why margins vary:** Competition (same product, 10 sellers), seasonality, category-specific Amazon fees, condition mismatches.

## Model 3: Print-on-Demand

### Entry & Operating Costs

```
SETUP:
  Printful/Teespring account:          €0
  Design (hire designer or DIY):       €200-500
  Initial ad spend (testing):          €300-500
  SUBTOTAL:                            €500-1,000

MONTHLY OPERATING:
  Platform fees:                       €0 (varies by sales volume)
  Ad spend (required to win):          €200-500
  Contingency:                         €100
  MONTHLY TOTAL:                       €300-600
```

### Margin Reality (2024-2026)

```
Example: Custom t-shirt

COST SIDE:
  Base product (Printful):             €8
  Platform fee (on print):             €1-2
  Fulfillment:                         €3 (shipping, handling)
  TOTAL PRODUCT COST:                  €12-11

REVENUE SIDE:
  Customer price:                      €25
  Platform take (commission):          -€7.50 (30%)
  Payment processor:                   -€0.75 (3%)
  NET PER UNIT:                        €6.75

MARGIN CALCULATION:
  Gross: (€25-€11) / €25 = 56%
  NET: €6.75 / €25 = 27%
  But with ad spend (CAC €5-10):       NEGATIVE until scale
```

### Why It's Risky at 1500€

```
Ad spend requirement:    €300-500/month (to win in crowded market)
Breakeven point:         Need 50-100 sales/month
Average conversion:      0.5-1% (need €30-60K traffic to reach 50 sales)
Timeline to profit:      6-12 months of ad spend before organic kicks in
Capital required:        €2,000-3,000 minimum

Verdict: Not recommended for 1500€ unless you have design skill (reduce freelance cost)
```

## Dropshipping (NOT RECOMMENDED for 1500€)

### Why Dropshipping Fails

```
SETUP COST:                             €1,200-1,500
  Shopify store:                        €29-99/month = €300-1,200
  App subscriptions:                    €50-200
  Initial ad spend:                     €500-1,000

MARGINS COLLAPSE:
  Gross margin target:                  30%
  Shopify fees:                         -6% (transaction + payment)
  Ad cost of customer:                  -15-20% (critical to break even)
  Supplier dropship rate:               -5-8% (handling, quality issues)
  Returns & chargebacks:                -3-5%
  NET MARGIN:                           5-8% (vs 15-30% arbitrage)

CASH FLOW TRAP:
  Customer pays today
  Supplier charges in 15-30 days
  Shopify hold funds 2-4 days
  You're funding inventory for 20-30 days with zero margin buffer

PROFITABILITY RATE:                     10-20% of stores succeed
FAILURE RATE:                           80-90% (mostly due to poor margins + ad waste)

Verdict: ❌ AVOID for 1500€ (better models exist with lower risk)
```

## Breakeven Analysis

### Retail Arbitrage

```
Fixed costs: €30/month
Per-unit contribution: €8-10 (20% margin on €25)

Breakeven units: €30 / €8 = 3-4 units/month
= 30-40 units at €25 = €750-1000/month revenue
= BREAKEVEN in week 1-2 of Month 1
```

### Digital Products

```
Fixed costs: €200/month (marketing + platform)
Per-unit contribution: €20 (80% margin on €25)

Breakeven units: €200 / €20 = 10 units/month
= Need 10 paying customers/month
= Roughly 1,000-2,000 website visitors (0.5-1% conversion)
= 2-3 weeks of ads + organic traffic
= BREAKEVEN in month 1-2
```

### Print-on-Demand

```
Fixed costs: €400/month (ads mandatory)
Per-unit contribution: €6.75 (27% margin on €25)

Breakeven units: €400 / €6.75 = 59 units/month
= Need 60+ sales/month
= Roughly €30-40K website traffic (1-2% conversion)
= Requires consistent €300-500/month ad spend
= BREAKEVEN in month 3-4 (if CAC remains low)
```
