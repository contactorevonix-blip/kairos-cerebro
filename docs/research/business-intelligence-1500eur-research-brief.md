## 2. RESEARCH QUESTIONS (ESTRUTURADO)

### 2.1 Financial Fundamentals
- [ ] **Unit Economics:** Qual é a margem mínima viável para 1500€? (target: >30%)
- [ ] **Breakeven:** Quantos dias/semanas até ROI em cada modelo?
- [ ] **Cash Flow:** Qual modelo tem ciclo mais rápido (cash in vs cash out)?
- [ ] **Scaling:** Como multiplica 1500€ → 5K€ → 15K€ sem risco?

### 2.2 Market Validation
- [ ] **Top 5 nichos:** Quais têm melhor demanda validada?
  - Marketplace arbitrage (eBay, Amazon, Aliexpress)
  - Wholesale + dropshipping
  - Liquidation/clearance stock
  - Digital products reselling
  - Niche e-commerce (Shopify dropship)
- [ ] **Margin reality:** Buy cost vs sell price real (não teórico)
- [ ] **Competition:** Como diferenciar-se vender "abaixo do mercado"?
- [ ] **Supplier reliability:** Tempo de restock, quality control

### 2.3 Operational Models
- [ ] **Model 1: Marketplace Arbitrage**
  - Setup: 0€ (usar eBay/Amazon existentes)
  - Inventory: Dropship (zero upfront)
  - Timeline: 7-14 dias per sale
  - Margin: 20-40%
  - Case studies: Real people who did this

- [ ] **Model 2: Wholesale + Local Resale**
  - Setup: 100-200€ (licença, conta bancária)
  - Inventory: Buy 50-100 units (1500€)
  - Timeline: 30-60 dias
  - Margin: 30-50%
  - Case studies: Where to source, local channels

- [ ] **Model 3: Digital Course Reselling**
  - Setup: 50-100€ (website basics)
  - Inventory: Digital (zero shipping)
  - Timeline: 3-7 dias
  - Margin: 60-80%
  - Case studies: Flávio Augusto pattern

- [ ] **Model 4: Liquidation Stock Flipping**
  - Setup: 200-300€ (sourcing network)
  - Inventory: Bulk clearance (1500€)
  - Timeline: 14-30 dias
  - Margin: 40-100%+ (if smart sourcing)
  - Case studies: Where liquidators source

- [ ] **Model 5: Niche Subscription/Membership**
  - Setup: 300€ (Gumroad, Circle, Mighty Networks)
  - Inventory: Content/expertise (zero production)
  - Timeline: 30-90 dias ramp
  - Margin: 70-90%
  - Case studies: Solo creators 1K→10K per month

### 2.4 Expert Frameworks

- [ ] **Naval leverage:** Qual é a multiplicação (tempo, capital, network)?
