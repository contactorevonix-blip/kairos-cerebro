## 💡 STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

### **Best Platforms by Use Case (2026)**

| Goal | Platform | Advantage | Fee |
|------|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Maximum reach** | Amazon FBA | Prime integration, global, 2-day delivery | 2.70-5.68/unit + 7-15% storage |
| **Maximum margin** | DTC (Shopify + 3PL) | No marketplace fees, brand control | 2.9% + 30¢ per order + 3PL ($5-10) |
| **Minimum risk** | Digital products | Zero inventory, zero fulfillment | Varies (30-50% platform cut) |
| **Speed to market** | Zero-MOQ platforms (Zendrop, CJDropshipping) | No upfront inventory | 10-20% per-unit premium |
| **International** | ShipMonk, ShipBob (3PL) | 10+ warehouses, customs handling | $1.50-3.00/unit + storage |

### **Margin Reality Check (2026)**

| Channel | Gross Margin | Net Margin (after all fees) | Notes |
|---------|-------------|--------------------------|-------|
| Amazon FBA | 50-70% | 10-25% | Peak season +8-15% fee surge |
| Shopify + 3PL | 50-70% | 20-35% | Best margins for scale |
| Digital products | 70-90% | 50-80% | No COGS, highest ROI |
| Dropshipping (zero-MOQ) | 50-80% | 10-25% | High fees eat margin |
| Retail arbitrage | 150-400% markup | 10-20% net | Inconsistent supply, gating risks |

### **Manufacturer Selection Framework**

**Best for low MOQ + high margin:**
1. Health & Beauty: 10-50 units, 35-55% margin → Alibaba, direct labs
2. Apparel: 50-200 units, 30-50% margin → Trendsi, direct factories
3. Home Goods: 5-100 units, 25-45% margin → Spocket, Oberlo
4. Pet Products: 20-100 units, 45-60% margin → Direct manufacturers
5. **AVOID:** Electronics (100+ MOQ, 10-20% margin), Food/Beverage (regulatory complexity)

- Tech accessories under €40 (70% difficulty, 15-24% margin)
- General home kitchen gadgets (>1000 reviews = saturated)
- Beach/apparel fashion (HIGH competition, +30% return rates)
- Consumer electronics (10-20% margin, price wars)

**SAFE ENTRY (2026):**
- Eco-friendly products (emerging, low saturation)
- Personalized/custom items (lower competition, niche defense)
- Patented products (IP moat, lowest saturation)
