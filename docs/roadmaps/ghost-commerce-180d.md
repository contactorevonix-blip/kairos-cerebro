# Ghost Commerce Omega - 180 Day Roadmap to 100k EUR MRR

## North Star

- Reach `100k EUR MRR` in 180 days via B2B2C white-label anti-fraud API.

## Phase 1 (Days 0-30): Foundation and Trust

- Deliver `/verify` API + API key auth + dashboard + landing.
- Ship localized risk context (country/language/currency/payments).
- Onboard first 10 design partners.
- KPI targets:
  - 5k verify calls/day (pilot traffic)
  - Precision proxy >= 85%
  - 2 paying pilots

## Phase 2 (Days 31-90): Product-Market Fit

- Add tenant configs (thresholds and action policies).
- Add partner analytics and audit logs.
- Launch SEO programmatic pages in top 5 markets.
- KPI targets:
  - 20 paying tenants
  - 25k verify calls/day
  - 25k EUR MRR

## Phase 3 (Days 91-140): Expansion and Automation

- Add bot integrations (Telegram/WhatsApp) and browser extension feed.
- Introduce usage-based metering and Stripe global billing packs.
- Roll out multilingual copy and region-specific conversion flows.
- KPI targets:
  - 60 paying tenants
  - 60k verify calls/day
  - 60k EUR MRR

## Phase 4 (Days 141-180): Scale to 100k

- Enterprise package (SLA + compliance exports + SIEM hooks).
- Channel partners (banks, PSPs, e-commerce platforms).
- Weekly growth loops and churn-reduction playbooks.
- KPI targets:
  - 100+ paying tenants
  - 100k verify calls/day
  - 100k EUR MRR

## Risk Controls

- False positives controlled by thresholding + review queue.
- Legal safety via explainable verdict language.
- Privacy by design and retention minimization.
