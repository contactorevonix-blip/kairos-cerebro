## Summary

Remove 6 misplaced hooks from `.claude/settings.local.json` that are registered under `UserPromptSubmit` without matchers. These hooks are:
- `config-change-audit.cjs` (belongs: `ConfigChange`)
- `post-tool-use-observer.cjs` (belongs: `PostToolUse`)
- `pre-commit-lint.cjs` (belongs: `PreToolUse(git commit)`)
- `precompact-wrapper.cjs` (belongs: `PreCompact`)
- `session-start.cjs` (belongs: `SessionStart`)
- `subagent-stop-observer.cjs` (belongs: `SubagentStop`)

All 6 are already correctly registered in `settings.json`. The `settings.local.json` duplicates are causing duplicate execution (~30–60s overhead per prompt).

**Impact:** CRITICAL — Performance degradation every prompt.

---

## Problem Statement

**Current State:**
- `.claude/settings.local.json` lines 66–119 contain 6 hook entries under `UserPromptSubmit`
- All 6 entries lack `matcher` field (required for proper filtering)
- All 6 are already in `settings.json` with correct hooks in correct events
- Result: Each prompt trigger executes hooks twice (once from correct event, once from UserPromptSubmit)

**What's Missing:**
- Removal of duplicate entries from settings.local.json

**Source:** AIOX-SYNC-AUDIT-2026-06-10.md, Gap 1.1 (lines 25–44)
