### Story Validation Checklist

@po runs 10-point check before @dev can implement:

1. **Title:** Clear, objective, unambiguous
2. **Description:** Problem and need explained (not solution)
3. **ACs:** Testable, preferably Given/When/Then format
4. **Scope:** IN (what's included) and OUT (what's explicitly excluded)
5. **Dependencies:** Prerequisite stories/resources listed
6. **Effort:** Story points or T-shirt size estimated
7. **Business Value:** Clear benefit to user or business
8. **Risks:** Potential problems identified
9. **Done Criteria:** Definition of "complete" clear
10. **Alignment:** Consistent with source PRD/epic

**Scoring:** ≥7/10 = GO (proceed to @dev), <7/10 = NO-GO (fixes required)

**Specification-Driven Determinism:**
