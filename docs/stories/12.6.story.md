### Story Creation Workflow

@sm follows `create-next-story.md` task:

1. **Read PRD:** Extract sharded requirements for this story
2. **Define Story:** Title, description, scope (IN/OUT), complexity
3. **Write ACs:** Acceptance criteria (Given/When/Then if possible)
4. **Identify Dependencies:** Prerequisite stories/resources
5. **Estimate Effort:** Story points or T-shirt size
6. **Document Context:** Reference to epic, PRD section, risk notes
7. **Save File:** `docs/stories/{EPIC-ID}.{num}.story.md`
8. **Checklist:** Run story-draft-checklist for quality assurance
