- **L2 (Templates):** Research brief template, evidence template
- **L3 (Config):** Research rules, evidence standards
- **L4 (Runtime):** @analyst operates on research tasks (not framework code)

**Task-First Contract:**
```

  Outputs: [gap analysis, evidence map, remediation paths]

```

### Research Workflow

@analyst follows research task:
1. **Read Gap:** Understand the gap description
2. **Research Evidence:** Find supporting information
3. **Analyze Remediation:** Identify fix paths (L4 or escalate L1/L2)
4. **Document Findings:** Structured output (gap-analysis.json)
5. **Trace to Testing:** Link gap → @qa test → Story 12.X

### Research Alignment

**No Invention** (research, EPIC-12 §5):
- @analyst researches, doesn't invent solutions
- Evidence-based recommendations

**Task-First Workflow:**
- Research is a task (inputs → outputs → success criteria)
- Results feed into PRDs/specs
