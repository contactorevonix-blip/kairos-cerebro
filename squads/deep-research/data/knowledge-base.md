# Knowledge Base — Deep Research Squad

## Core Frameworks

### SALSA (Booth)
Search → AppraisaL → Synthesis → Analysis
4 fases obrigatórias. Nenhuma pode ser saltada.

### 14 Review Types (Grant & Booth)
Systematic · Scoping · Rapid · Critical · Meta-analysis · Realist ·
Umbrella · Integrative · Mapping · Qualitative Systematic · State-of-the-art ·
Systematized · Mixed Studies · Literature Review

Regra: review type determina synthesis approach.
Scoping ≠ Systematic. Scoping = exploratório. Systematic = teste de hipótese.

### STARLITE (Booth)
S-ampling · T-ype of study · A-pproaches · R-ange of years ·
L-imits · I-nclusion/exclusion · T-erms · E-lectronic sources

### PICO / SPIDER
- PICO: questões quantitativas/intervenções
- SPIDER: questões qualitativas/experiências

### PPV (Ioannidis)
"Why Most Published Research Findings Are False"
PPV = (1-β)R / (R - βR + α)
Bias detectado → confiança reduzida

### System 1 / System 2 (Kahneman)
- System 1: rápido, intuitivo, propenso a bias
- System 2: lento, analítico, deliberado
Audit obrigatório de cognitive biases antes de recomendar.
